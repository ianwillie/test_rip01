THE NEWSLETTER OF PAISLEY & DISTRICT SHRG   NO. 6

OPEN DAY SUCCESS!

Yes! with a turnout of over forty including representatives from from the churches, Renfrew District Council, the young liberals, the telephone Samaritans, catholic marriage advisory service, Paisley Daily express, The Sunday Standard and our own NEC, not forgetting the Edinburgh swithboard staff who we thank for their help during the question time, and to our guest speaker Mr. David Jago, again our thanks. we definately say it was a success.

TREASURER TAKES DRAMATICALLY
ILL AT COMMITTEE MEETING>
As a result of a proposal by the branch chairman to lower the tea and subs. money from 40p to a set rate of 30p for all, the treasurer could be seen to turn white and visibly collapse at the shock, especially when the motion was carried. Unfortunately the treasurer could not vote against his horrid, vile, disgusting and sickening motion, having been struck dumb (no comments please) when originally proposed.
